My research interests relate to the overall group focus on program verification, where I was particularly interested in the parameterization of SMT solvers. I have since switched to working on problems closer to the theoretical side of parameterization, while staying open to applications in other fields of CS. I have also worked in probabilistic cache management, abstract interpretation and Markov decision processes.
